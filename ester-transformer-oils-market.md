<p><p>Ester transformer oils are classified into two main types: natural ester transformer fluid and synthetic ester transformer oil. Natural ester transformer fluid is derived from vegetable oils, making it biodegradable and environmentally friendly. On the other hand, synthetic ester transformer oil is chemically synthesized and offers specific performance advantages such as enhanced oxidation stability and low temperature properties. Both types of ester transformer oils are commonly used in power transformers to improve overall efficiency and safety in electrical systems.</p></p>
