Cables with 1.00 mm pitches offer a balance between conductor density and manageable cable size. Cables with 1.25 mm pitches have connectors spaced further apart, allowing for larger conductor size and higher current-carrying capacity. These variations in pitch cater to different application requirements, enabling flexible flat cables to be used in diverse industries such as consumer electronics, automotive, and healthcare.</p></p>
