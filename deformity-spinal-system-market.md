<p><p>The spinal deformity system market encompasses various types of medical devices used in the treatment of spinal deformities. Rods are used to provide stability and support to the spine. Hooks are attached to the vertebrae to facilitate correction and alignment. Plates are fixed to the spine to promote fusion of the vertebrae. Cages are placed between vertebrae to restore disc height and improve stability. Pedicle screws are inserted into the vertebrae to connect implants and provide additional stability. These devices play a crucial role in the management of spinal deformities.</p></p>
