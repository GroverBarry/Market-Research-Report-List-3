<p><p>Horizontal drying tunnels are used in industries where the drying process requires the movement of materials horizontally. These tunnels have a conveyor belt system that moves the materials through the drying chamber. Vertical drying tunnels, on the other hand, are designed for industries where the drying process requires the movement of materials vertically. These tunnels typically have a system that lifts the materials up or down through the drying chamber. The choice between horizontal and vertical drying tunnels depends on the specific requirements of the industry and the materials being dried.</p></p>
