<p><p>The projector hangers market can be classified into two types: adjustable and non-adjustable. Adjustable projector hangers allow users to change the height and angle of the projector, offering flexibility in positioning the projected image. This type is suitable for environments where the alignment needs frequent adjustments. On the other hand, non-adjustable projector hangers have a fixed position and cannot be modified once installed. They are commonly used in permanent installations where the projector alignment is set and does not require any changes. These different types cater to different needs and preferences in the projector hangers market.</p></p>
