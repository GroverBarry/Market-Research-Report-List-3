<p><p>The Cartridge Fuses market consists of three types: Fast Blow, Medium/Normal Blow, and Time Delay/Slow Blow. Fast Blow fuses have a quick response time and are designed to protect circuits from short-circuits, ensuring rapid disruption of electrical flow. Medium/Normal Blow fuses provide protection against overcurrent and short-circuits, offering a moderate response time. Time Delay/Slow Blow fuses have a delayed response time and are ideal for circuits with high inrush current at startup. These fuses prevent unnecessary tripping during temporary overloads while providing protection against short-circuits.</p></p>
