<p><p>A podiatry insole, also known as a foot orthotic, is a medical device designed to provide support and alleviate pain or discomfort in the feet. Additionally, the growing aging population, which is more prone to foot ailments, is further fueling market growth.</p><p>Moreover, the rising awareness about foot health and the importance of maintaining proper foot alignment and posture is boosting the adoption of podiatry insoles.
