<p><p>Nanotechnology drug delivery systems have revolutionized drug delivery by offering various types of carriers. Nanocrystals are solid particles that enhance drug solubility. Nanoparticles encapsulate drugs within their tiny structures and efficiently deliver them to the targeted site. Dendrimers are highly branched molecules that create a controlled drug release. Gold nanoparticles possess unique properties for drug loading and targeting. Fullerenes are spherical carbon molecules that trap and transport drugs. Liposomes create lipid bilayers to enclose drugs for targeted drug release. Nanotubes intercalate drugs within their cylindrical structures. Other types of carriers, such as magnetic nanoparticles and quantum dots, also provide innovative drug delivery approaches.</p></p>
