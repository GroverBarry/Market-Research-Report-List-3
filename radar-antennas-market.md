<p><strong>The Radar Antennas Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Half Wave Dipole</li><li>Quarter Wave Dipole</li><li>Uniform Linear Array Antenna</li><li>Non-Uniform Array Antenna</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Radar antennas are classified into various types based on their design and functionality. The half wave dipole antenna is a simple, efficient antenna that radiates or receives signals equally in all directions. The quarter wave dipole antenna is similar but offers a more compact size. The uniform linear array antenna consists of multiple elements arranged in a straight line, providing enhanced directivity. In contrast, a non-uniform array antenna has elements with varying spacing, enabling adjustable beamforming capabilities. These different types cater to the diverse needs of the radar antennas market.</p></p>
