<p><strong>The Long-Range Lidars Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Line Lidars</li><li>Multi-Line Lidars</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Long-Range Lidars are categorized into two types: Single Line Lidars and Multi-Line Lidars. Single Line Lidars emit a single laser beam in one direction, scanning its surroundings in a linear manner, making them suitable for applications that require a limited field of view. On the other hand, Multi-Line Lidars emit multiple laser beams simultaneously, allowing for a wider field of view and capturing more data points. These lidars are utilized in applications that require a larger coverage area and higher resolution, such as autonomous driving and robotics.</p></p>
