HID lighting is commonly used for outdoor and industrial applications due to its high intensity and long lifespan.</p><p>Plasma and Induction Lighting: These lighting technologies use a combination of gases and electromagnetic fields to produce light. Plasma lighting utilizes excited gases, while induction lighting uses electromagnetic fields to excite mercury vapor and produce light.</p><p>Fluorescent Lighting: Fluorescent lighting uses low-pressure mercury vapor to produce light when an electrical current passes through it. This lighting technology is commonly used in residential, commercial, and industrial settings due to its energy efficiency and long lifespan.</p><p>Others Market: This category includes other energy-efficient lighting technologies that are not mentioned above.
