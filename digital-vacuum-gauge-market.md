<p><p>The digital vacuum gauge market includes two main types: analog and digital. Analog vacuum gauges use mechanical components to measure vacuum levels and display them on a dial or gauge. On the other hand, digital vacuum gauges use electronic sensors to accurately measure and display vacuum levels on a screen. Analog gauges offer a traditional and straightforward approach, while digital gauges provide more precise readings and often offer additional features like data logging and connectivity options. Both types cater to different user needs and preferences in the vacuum gauge market.</p></p>
