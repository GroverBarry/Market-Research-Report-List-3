<p><p>A laser projector keyboard is a virtual keyboard that projects a full-size keyboard onto a flat surface using a laser beam. The user can then type on the projected keyboard as if it were a physical keyboard. This technology uses sensors to detect which keys are being pressed and translates that into keystrokes on a connected device such as a smartphone, tablet, or computer.</p><p>The current outlook of the laser projector keyboard market is quite positive. The rising adoption of advanced technology, increasing use of smartphones and tablets, and the need for portable and compact input devices are major factors driving the demand for laser projector keyboards.
