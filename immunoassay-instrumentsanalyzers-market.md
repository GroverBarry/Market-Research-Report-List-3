<p><p>Immunoassay instruments/analyzers market types include chemiluminescence immunoassay, immunofluorescence analyzers, radioimmunoassay, and enzyme-linked immunoassay. Chemiluminescence immunoassay uses chemiluminescent reactions to detect antibodies, while immunofluorescence analyzers use fluorescent markers to identify antibodies. Radioimmunoassay involves the use of radioactive tracers for detection, and enzyme-linked immunoassay utilizes enzymes for signal generation. Additionally, there is a consumables market for immunoassay instruments, where components like reagents and kits are sold for testing purposes.</p></p>
