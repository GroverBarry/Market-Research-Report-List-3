<p><p>The Smart Beacon market consists of two main types: Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) and Hybrid. BLE beacons are small, wireless devices that use low-power Bluetooth technology to transmit signals to nearby devices, enabling location-based services and personalized marketing. Hybrid beacons, on the other hand, combine BLE technology with other communication technologies like Wi-Fi or cellular networks to offer enhanced functionalities such as increased range or data collection capabilities. Both types of beacons are widely used in various industries to improve customer engagement, indoor navigation, and asset tracking.</p></p>
