<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The rotary drilling bits market consists of two types: roller-cone bits and fixed-cutter bits. Roller-cone bits are composed of three cones with teeth that roll against the rock surface, allowing for effective drilling in various formations. On the other hand, fixed-cutter bits feature polycrystalline diamond compact or natural diamond cutters, which provide a more consistent and precise drilling performance. Both types serve different purposes and are used in oil and gas exploration, mining, and construction industries, depending on the specific drilling requirements.</p></p>
