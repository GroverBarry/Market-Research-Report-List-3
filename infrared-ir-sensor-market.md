<p><p>The Infrared (IR) Sensor market includes two main types: the Thermal Infrared (TIR) Sensor market and the Quantum Infrared (QIR) Sensor market. TIR Sensors detect and measure thermal energy emitted by objects, allowing accurate temperature measurements and thermal imaging. On the other hand, QIR Sensors use quantum technology to detect infrared radiation and offer higher sensitivity and precision compared to TIR sensors. Both TIR and QIR Sensors have applications in various industries like automotive, aerospace, and healthcare for tasks like night vision, temperature monitoring, and object detection.</p></p>
