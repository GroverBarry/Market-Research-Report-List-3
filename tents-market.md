<p><p>The tent market is divided into two main types: 3-season and 4-season tents. 3-season tents are designed for use in spring, summer, and fall, offering good ventilation and protection against rain and mild weather conditions. However, they may not withstand heavy snow or extreme cold. On the other hand, 4-season tents are built to withstand harsh winter conditions, including heavy snow and strong winds. They provide better insulation and stability but may be heavier and more expensive than 3-season tents. The choice of tent depends on the desired season of use and expected weather conditions.</p></p>
