<p><p>The Liquid Crystal on Silicon (LCoS) based Spatial Light Modulator (SLM) market can be classified into two types: Reflective Spatial Light Modulators and Transmissive Spatial Light Modulators. Reflective SLMs utilize a reflective surface to manipulate light, offering high resolution and bright images. Transmissive SLMs, on the other hand, function by modulating light passing through the liquid crystal layer. These types provide different functionalities and are used in various applications such as projectors, holography, and optical communications.</p></p>
