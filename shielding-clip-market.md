The Dual Clip is utilized for securing multiple cables or wires together, simplifying cable management and providing additional shielding capabilities. Lastly, the Single Clip is a versatile option used for general cable fastening purposes, enabling easy installation and reliable cable shielding in various electronic applications. These clips offer efficient shielding solutions for different cable management needs in electronic devices.</p></p>
