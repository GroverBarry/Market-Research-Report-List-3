<p><p>The Liquid Transfer Pumps Market includes three main types of transfer pumps: AC Transfer Pump, DC Transfer Pump, and Hand Transfer Pump. AC Transfer Pumps are powered by alternating current, commonly used for larger scale applications and pumping high volumes of liquid. DC Transfer Pumps, on the other hand, run on direct current and are typically used in smaller and more portable systems. Lastly, Hand Transfer Pumps are operated manually and are ideal for smaller tasks or areas where power supply is limited. These pumps cater to various needs and preferences in liquid transfer operations.</p></p>
