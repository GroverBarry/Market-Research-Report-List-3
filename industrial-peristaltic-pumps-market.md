<p><p>The industrial peristaltic pumps market comprises two main types: peristaltic tube pumps and peristaltic hose pumps. Peristaltic tube pumps utilize a flexible tube that is squeezed and released by rotating rollers, creating a pumping action. These pumps are commonly used in low-flow applications and are ideal for handling corrosive fluids. On the other hand, peristaltic hose pumps feature a flexible hose and external shoe, which compresses the hose against a solid housing. These pumps are commonly used for high-pressure applications and are suitable for abrasive fluids or shear-sensitive materials.</p></p>
