<p><p>The magnetic beads market can be categorized into various types. Silica magnetic beads are made of silica nanoparticles coated with a magnetic material, providing a high binding capacity for biomolecules. Agarose magnetic beads are derived from agarose gel, offering excellent stability and compatibility with biological samples. Polymer magnetic beads are composed of a polymer matrix with embedded magnetic nanoparticles, ensuring high chemical stability and versatility. Lastly, the "others" category includes various specialized magnetic beads designed for specific applications, such as protein purification or DNA isolation. Each type provides unique advantages for different research and diagnostic purposes.</p></p>
