<p><p>The heart valve market consists of three main types: Transcatheter Heart Valves, Tissue Heart Valves, and Mechanical Heart Valves. Transcatheter heart valves are inserted through a catheter and are suitable for patients who cannot undergo open-heart surgery. Tissue heart valves are made from biological tissues and are more compatible with the patient's body, but may require replacement over time. Mechanical heart valves are artificial valves made from materials like metal or ceramic, offering durability but requiring lifelong use of blood-thinning medications.</p></p>
