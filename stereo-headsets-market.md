<p><p>The stereo headsets market is divided into four main types: On-Ear Headsets, Over-Ear Headsets, Earbuds, and In-Ear Headsets. On-Ear Headsets rest on the external part of the ears, providing a comfortable listening experience. Over-Ear Headsets cover the entire ear, offering enhanced noise isolation and sound quality. Earbuds fit snugly in the ear canal and are highly portable, making them popular for exercising. In-Ear Headsets are similar to earbuds but go deeper into the ear canal, providing better noise isolation and audio fidelity. Each type caters to different preferences and usage scenarios in the audio market.</p></p>
