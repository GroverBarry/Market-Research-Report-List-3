<p><strong>The Light Curing Acrylic Adhesive Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>UV Light Curing Acrylic Adhesive</li><li>Visible Light Curing Acrylic Adhesive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Light curing acrylic adhesives are categorized into two main types based on the type of light used for curing: UV light curing acrylic adhesives and visible light curing acrylic adhesives. UV light curing acrylic adhesives are cured using ultraviolet light, which initiates a photochemical reaction to harden the adhesive. On the other hand, visible light curing acrylic adhesives are cured using visible light, typically in the blue light spectrum, to initiate the curing process. Both types offer fast curing times and high bond strength for various applications.</p></p>
