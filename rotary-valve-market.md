<p><p>The rotary valve market consists of different types of valves, including butterfly valves, ball valves, and plug valves. Butterfly valves are often used in large-scale industrial applications as they provide efficient control of fluid flow. Ball valves are commonly used in applications where tight shut-off is required, using a spherical closure to control flow. Plug valves, on the other hand, have a cylindrical or conical plug that can be rotated to control flow. These valves play a crucial role in regulating the flow of liquids or gases in various industries.</p></p>
