<p><p>Membrane Bioreactor (MBR) Systems are a type of wastewater treatment technology that combine biological processes with membrane filtration. The MBR system market is categorized into three types: hollow fiber, flat sheet, and multi tubular. Hollow fiber systems use a bundle of porous hollow fibers to separate solids from liquids, while flat sheet systems use a flat sheet membrane to filter wastewater. Multi tubular systems consist of multiple tubular membranes that remove contaminants. Each type offers unique advantages and is utilized based on specific project requirements and treatment objectives.</p></p>
