<p><p>Accumulators are devices used to store small amounts of fluid under high pressure for future use. There are four main types of accumulators in the market. The weight-loaded piston type uses gravity to compress the fluid, while the diaphragm (bladder) type uses a rubber-like bladder to contain the fluid. The spring type utilizes a spring to compress the fluid, and the hydro-pneumatic piston type employs a combination of hydraulic and pneumatic pressure to store the fluid. Each of these types has its own unique features and applications in various industries.</p></p>
