<p><p>Ceramic sand screens are used in the oil and gas industry for sand control in well completions. There are three types of ceramic sand screens: roller ceramic sand screens, water washing roller ceramic sand screens, and vibro ceramic sand screens. Roller ceramic sand screens are equipped with rollers that help remove fine particles from the wellbore. Water washing roller ceramic sand screens use water to clean the sand and prevent sand accumulation. Vibro ceramic sand screens use vibration to prevent sand blockage and increase flow efficiency in well completions.</p></p>
