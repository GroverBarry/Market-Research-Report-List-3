<p><strong>The Underground Mining Vehicles Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Longwall</li><li>Room and Pillar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The underground mining vehicles market consists of various types, including Longwall and Room and Pillar. Longwall mining involves the extraction of minerals in a continuous panel, typically using a shearer to remove the material. This method is suitable for thick coal seams. On the other hand, Room and Pillar mining creates a network of empty spaces within the coal seam, leaving pillars for support. It is ideal for thinner seams or areas where the coal is too deep. These different methods cater to specific mining conditions and operational requirements in the industry.</p></p>
