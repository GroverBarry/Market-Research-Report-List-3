<p><p>The UV water purifier market consists of various types, two of which are the tapered water purifier and the self-cleaning water purifier. On the other hand, self-cleaning water purifiers integrate automatic cleaning mechanisms that prevent the build-up of contaminants and ensure sustained performance over time. These types of UV water purifiers cater to different consumer preferences and provide effective solutions for water purification needs.</p></p>
