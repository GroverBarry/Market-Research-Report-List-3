<p><p>The Head-Mount Temperature Transmitters market includes two main types - Thermocouples and Resistance Temperature Detectors (RTDs). Thermocouples are temperature sensors that use two different metals to create a voltage proportional to the temperature difference between their ends. They offer wide temperature ranges and are suitable for harsh environments. On the other hand, RTDs use the principle of electrical resistance to measure temperature. They have high accuracy and stability but limited temperature ranges. Both types are commonly used in various industries for temperature measurements and control purposes.</p></p>
