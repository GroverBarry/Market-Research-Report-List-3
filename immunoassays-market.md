<p><p>Immunoassays are analytical techniques used to detect and quantify specific substances in biological samples. The market for immunoassays includes various types such as enzyme immunoassay, fluorescence immunoassay, chemiluminescence immunoassay, radioimmunoassay, and nephelometric immunoassay. </p><p>Enzyme immunoassay utilizes enzyme-labeled antibodies to detect target substances. Fluorescence immunoassay employs fluorescent dyes to generate a signal. Chemiluminescence immunoassay uses light-emitting reactions for detection. Radioimmunoassay measures radioactive tracers to identify substances. Nephelometric immunoassay uses light scattering to measure substance concentration. Each type has its own advantages and applications, catering to the diverse needs of diagnostic laboratories, research institutions, and the healthcare industry.</p></p>
