<p><strong>The Touch Screen Controllers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Resistive Touch Controllers</li><li>Capacitive Touch Controllers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The touch screen controllers market comprises two major types, resistive touch controllers and capacitive touch controllers. Resistive touch controllers use a layered panel to measure changes in electrical current, allowing users to interact with the screen through applying pressure. On the other hand, capacitive touch controllers rely on the conductive properties of the human body to detect and respond to touches. These controllers have different mechanisms but serve the same purpose of facilitating user interactions with touch screen devices such as smartphones, tablets, and ATMs.</p></p>
