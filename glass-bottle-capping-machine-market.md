<p><p>The glass bottle capping machine market offers three types of machines: fully automatic, semi-automatic, and manual. Fully automatic machines require minimal human intervention as they perform the entire capping process automatically. Semi-automatic machines demand some human assistance, such as placement and removal of bottles, while the actual capping process is automated. Manual machines, on the other hand, rely entirely on human labor to cap each bottle individually. These different market types cater to varying production needs, offering flexibility and scalability to businesses involved in bottle capping operations.</p></p>
