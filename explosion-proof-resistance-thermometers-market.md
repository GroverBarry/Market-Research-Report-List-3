<p><strong>The Explosion Proof Resistance Thermometers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>2 Wire</li><li>3 Wire</li><li>4 Wire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Explosion Proof Resistance Thermometers are classified into different market types based on the wiring connection: 2 Wire, 3 Wire, and 4 Wire configurations. In a 2 Wire system, the resistance thermometer and connecting cable share the same wires for current supply and signal output. The 3 Wire system employs one additional wire to compensate for wire resistance, resulting in more accurate temperature measurements. The 4 Wire system further enhances accuracy by using two additional wires, with separate paths for current supply and signal output, effectively eliminating wire resistance.</p></p>
