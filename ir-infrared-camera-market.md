<p><strong>The IR (Infrared) Camera Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Germanium</li><li>Silicon</li><li>Zinc Selenide</li><li>Sapphire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>IR (Infrared) cameras are classified based on the type of material used for the lens. Germanium lenses are commonly used in IR cameras due to their high refractive index and good transmission in the infrared spectrum. Silicon lenses are cost-effective but have limited transmission in the IR range. Zinc Selenide lenses offer excellent transmission in the mid-wave and long-wave infrared spectra. Sapphire lenses are known for their durability and scratch resistance. Each type of lens material caters to different needs and applications in the IR camera market.</p></p>
