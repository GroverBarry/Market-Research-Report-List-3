<p><p>The laser lamps market comprises two main types: pulsed and continuous. Pulsed laser lamps emit light in short bursts, providing high intensity for a brief period of time. They are commonly used in applications that require precise and controlled energy delivery, such as laser cutting and welding. Continuous laser lamps emit a continuous beam of light and are commonly used in applications like laser engraving, medical surgery, and scientific research. Both types have their own advantages and are used extensively in various industries.</p></p>
