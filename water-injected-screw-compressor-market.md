<p><p>The Water Injected Screw Compressor market consists of two main types: Positive Displacement Compressors and Speed Compressors. Positive Displacement Compressors work by drawing in air and reducing its volume through a rotating mechanism, resulting in increased pressure. On the other hand, Speed Compressors rely on high-speed impellers to create a continuous flow of air, generating higher pressure. Both types use water injection to cool the compressed air, improving efficiency and reducing energy consumption. These innovations contribute to the growing market demand for Water Injected Screw Compressors.</p></p>
