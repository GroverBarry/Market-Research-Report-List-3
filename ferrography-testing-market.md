<p><strong>The Ferrography Testing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Analytical Ferrography</li><li>Direct-Reading Ferrography</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ferrography testing refers to a technique used to analyze the wear and tear of machinery. One type is analytical ferrography, which involves collecting a sample of oil from the machinery and analyzing it under a microscope to identify the type and severity of wear particles. The other type is direct-reading ferrography, where a specialized sensor is installed in the machinery to continuously monitor the wear debris in real-time and provide instant feedback on the condition of the equipment. Both methods are valuable for maintenance professionals in predicting machinery failures and optimizing maintenance schedules.</p></p>
