<p><p>The emollient market can be classified into two types: synthetic and natural oil-based emollients. Natural oil-based emollients, on the other hand, are derived from plant or vegetable sources and contain natural compounds that provide moisturizing and softening effects to the skin. They are generally considered to be more sustainable and environmentally friendly. Both types of emollients are used in various skincare and cosmetic products.</p></p>
