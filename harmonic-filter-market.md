<p><strong>The Harmonic Filter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Harmonic Filter</li><li>Passive Harmonic Filter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Harmonic Filter market consists of two main types: Active Harmonic Filters and Passive Harmonic Filters. Active Harmonic Filters are electronic devices that actively detect and remove harmonics generated by non-linear loads, ensuring a cleaner and more efficient power quality. On the other hand, Passive Harmonic Filters are composed of passive components like resistors, capacitors, and inductors that absorb and filter out harmonics from the electrical system. Both types help in reducing harmonic distortion, preventing equipment malfunction, and optimizing energy consumption.</p></p>
