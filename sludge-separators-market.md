<p><p>Sludge separators are used to separate solid particulates from liquid waste streams. There are two main types of sludge separators: vertical and horizontal. Vertical sludge separators are designed with a vertical orientation, allowing the sludge to settle at the bottom and be pumped out through an outlet. Horizontal sludge separators, on the other hand, are horizontally oriented and allow the sludge to settle on the bottom before being removed. Both types of separators are commonly used in various industries to efficiently treat and manage sludge waste.</p></p>
