AC motors operate using alternating current and are commonly used in high-speed electric trains due to their ability to generate high power outputs. DC motors, on the other hand, operate using direct current and are typically used in electric vehicles and low-speed trains. Both types of motors play a significant role in the electric traction market, albeit offering different advantages and applications.</p></p>
