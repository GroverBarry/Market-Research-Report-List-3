<p><p>The vehicle diesel engine market is categorized into three types based on the number of cylinders: 4-cylinder, 6-cylinder, and above 6-cylinder. On the other hand, a 6-cylinder diesel engine offers more power and torque, making it suitable for larger vehicles and heavy-duty applications. Lastly, above 6-cylinder diesel engines are usually found in commercial trucks, buses, and industrial machinery, providing maximum power output and performance for demanding tasks.</p></p>
