<p><p>Antimicrobial dressings, such as Silver Wound Dressing, Iodine Antimicrobial Wound Dressing, Honey Antimicrobial Wound Dressing, and Polyhexamethylene Biguanide (PHMB), are used in wound care to prevent or treat infections. Silver dressings release silver ions that have antimicrobial properties, killing bacteria in the wound. Iodine dressings use iodine to kill pathogens. Honey dressings utilize the natural antibacterial properties of honey. PHMB dressings contain a synthetic antimicrobial agent that helps control bacterial growth. These dressings provide effective protection against infection and promote wound healing.</p></p>
