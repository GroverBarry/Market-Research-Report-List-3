<p><p>The mountain bike front fork market is divided into two types: air spring suspension forks and coil spring suspension forks. Air spring suspension forks use compressed air to absorb shocks and provide a smooth ride. They are lightweight and allow riders to adjust the fork's sag and rebound according to their preferences. On the other hand, coil spring suspension forks use a metal coil to absorb shocks. They provide a more linear and consistent feel but are generally heavier and have limited adjustability options.</p></p>
