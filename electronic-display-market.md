<p><p>The electronic display market is segmented into three major types: LCD (liquid crystal display), LED (light-emitting diode), and OLED (organic light-emitting diode). LCDs utilize liquid crystals to form images, making them cost-effective with good image quality. LEDs use a backlighting system to produce images, offering better brightness and energy efficiency than LCDs. OLEDs employ organic compounds to emit light when an electric current passes through them, resulting in high contrast ratios, wider viewing angles, and thinner displays. Each display type caters to different needs and technological advancements within the electronic industry.</p></p>
