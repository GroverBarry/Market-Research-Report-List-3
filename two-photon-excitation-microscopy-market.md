<p><p>Two-photon excitation microscopy is a powerful imaging technique that allows for deeper tissue penetration and reduced photodamage compared to traditional microscopy methods. In the market, there are two main types of two-photon excitation microscopes: Two-photon upright microscopes and Two-photon inverted microscopes. Two-photon upright microscopes are ideal for imaging thick samples or tissues that require a longer working distance, while two-photon inverted microscopes are suitable for imaging live cells or tissues in culture dishes with higher resolution and precision.</p></p>
