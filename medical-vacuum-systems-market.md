<p><strong>The Medical Vacuum Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dry Claw Pump Techology</li><li>Dry Rotary Vane Technology</li><li>Oil Sealed Rotary Vane Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Medical Vacuum Systems market is segmented into three types: Dry Claw Pump Technology, Dry Rotary Vane Technology, and Oil Sealed Rotary Vane Technology. Dry Claw Pump Technology uses claw-shaped rotors to create a vacuum without the need for oil lubrication, making it more efficient and environmentally friendly. Dry Rotary Vane Technology utilizes rotating vanes to maintain a vacuum, without the use of oil, making it suitable for medical applications. Oil Sealed Rotary Vane Technology utilizes rotating vanes that are sealed with oil, providing a reliable vacuum system commonly used in medical facilities.</p></p>
