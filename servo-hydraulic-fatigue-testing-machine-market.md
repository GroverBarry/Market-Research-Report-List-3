<p><p>The servo hydraulic fatigue testing machine market consists of two main types: dynamic servo hydraulic fatigue testing machines and static servo hydraulic fatigue testing machines. </p><p>Dynamic servo hydraulic fatigue testing machines are designed to apply cyclic loads to test materials and components under real-life conditions. These machines simulate dynamic forces and vibrations to evaluate the fatigue life of the tested samples. </p><p>In contrast, static servo hydraulic fatigue testing machines apply constant loads for extended periods to study the long-term behavior and durability of materials and components. They are used to assess the structural integrity and stability of various products.</p></p>
