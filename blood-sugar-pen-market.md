<p><p>The Blood Sugar Pen market comprises two main types: Reusable Type and Disposable Type. Reusable Type pens are designed for long-term use as they can be refilled with a new supply of insulin after each use. These pens are cost-effective and environmentally friendly. On the other hand, Disposable Type pens are intended for single-use and come pre-filled with a fixed amount of insulin. This type offers convenience as there is no need for insulin cartridge changes or refilling. However, they are relatively more expensive and generate more waste compared to the reusable type.</p></p>
