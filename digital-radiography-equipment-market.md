<p><ul><li>CR Tech Digital X-Ray System</li><li>DR Tech Digital X-Ray System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Digital Radiography Equipment market can be categorized into two types: CR Tech Digital X-Ray System and DR Tech Digital X-Ray System. CR Tech Digital X-Ray System uses a cassette-based technology, where the X-Ray image is captured on a plate inside the cassette, which is then processed and digitized. On the other hand, DR Tech Digital X-Ray System captures the X-Ray image directly onto a flat panel detector, eliminating the need for cassettes. Both technologies provide high-quality digital images for medical diagnostic purposes but differ in the way the images are captured and processed.</p></p>
