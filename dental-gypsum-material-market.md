<p><p>The dental gypsum material market is composed of three main types: dental plaster, model dental stone, and die dental stone. Dental plaster is a white powder used for preliminary impressions and casts. Model dental stone, also known as Type III dental stone, is used for making accurate models, and has a higher strength than dental plaster. Die dental stone, or Type IV dental stone, is used for making precise dental dies or models and has improved resistance to abrasion. These materials play crucial roles in dentistry by producing accurate dental casts and models for various dental procedures.</p></p>
