<p><p>A horizontal peeler centrifuge is a type of centrifuge machine used for solid-liquid separation in various industries such as chemical, pharmaceutical, food, and wastewater treatment. The liquid is then discharged through an outlet, leaving behind the solid cake.</p><p>The market for horizontal peeler centrifuges is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5% during the forecast period.
