<p><p>The bio-protein drug market can be categorized into three main types: monoclonal antibodies, therapeutic proteins, and vaccines. Monoclonal antibodies are laboratory-created proteins designed to target specific disease-causing agents in the body. Therapeutic proteins are proteins that are naturally present in the body or are artificially developed to treat diseases or disorders. Vaccines are biological substances that stimulate the immune system to produce immunity against specific diseases. All three types play crucial roles in the field of biotechnology and are used in the treatment and prevention of various diseases.</p></p>
