<p><p>A fanless PC, also known as a passively cooled PC, is a computer system that operates without the use of a fan for cooling. Instead, it relies on passive cooling mechanisms such as heat sinks, heat pipes, and convection to dissipate heat generated by the internal components. Fanless PCs are typically compact and quiet, making them suitable for a variety of applications such as industrial automation, digital signage, healthcare, transportation, and home entertainment.</p><p>The market for fanless PCs has been witnessing healthy growth in recent years. The increasing demand for energy-efficient and silent computing solutions, coupled with the rising adoption of fanless PCs in various industrial sectors, has been driving market growth.
