<p><p>Ophthalmic viscoelastic agents are substances used in eye surgeries to maintain the space between the cornea and iris. Cohesive viscoelastic agents have higher molecular weight, providing a more cohesive gel-like consistency for longer time. Low viscosity dispersion viscoelastic agents, on the other hand, have lower molecular weight and offer a more aqueous solution. These agents have different properties and applications during eye surgeries, ensuring effective lubrication, protection, and stabilization of ocular tissues.</p></p>
