<p><p>The saw spindle moulder market includes two main types of saws: horizontal saws and vertical saws. Horizontal saws are designed with a horizontal orientation, where the workpiece is placed on a table and pushed against the saw blade. These saws are typically used for cutting long, wide pieces of wood. On the other hand, vertical saws have a vertical orientation, with the blade positioned above the workpiece. These saws are ideal for cutting smaller, intricate shapes and are commonly used in woodworking and crafting applications.</p></p>
