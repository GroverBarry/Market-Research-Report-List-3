<p><p>Digital Power ICs are integrated circuits that use digital technology for power management and control purposes. The market for digital power ICs can be divided into two main types: Digital Power Management (DPM) and Digital Power Control (DPC). </p><p>DPM refers to the use of digital technology to efficiently manage and distribute power in electronic devices. This involves monitoring and optimizing power consumption, enabling better energy efficiency and improved performance.</p><p>DPC, on the other hand, focuses on the digital control of power delivery. It involves using digital algorithms and techniques to regulate voltage, current, and other power parameters to ensure stable and reliable operation of electronic systems.</p></p>
