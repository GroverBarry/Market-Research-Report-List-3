<p><p>A separatory funnel, also known as a separating funnel, is a laboratory apparatus used in liquid-liquid extraction to separate immiscible liquids of different densities. It consists of a conical glass or plastic vessel with a cylindrical neck and a stopcock at the bottom. The immiscible liquids are added to the funnel, and after mixing, they separate into two distinct layers due to their different densities. The stopcock is then opened to drain the lower layer, allowing for the separation of the two liquids.</p><p>The future of the separatory funnel market appears promising, with steady growth expected in the coming years.
