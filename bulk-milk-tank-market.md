<p><strong>The Bulk Milk Tank Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Horizontal Closed Tank</li><li>Silo/ Vertical Closed Tank</li><li>Open Tank</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bulk milk tanks are used for storing and cooling milk in the dairy industry. There are three main types of bulk milk tanks, namely horizontal closed tanks, silo/vertical closed tanks, and open tanks. Horizontal closed tanks are compact and space-saving, suitable for small-scale operations. Silo/vertical closed tanks are larger and can hold a significant amount of milk, ideal for medium to large-scale dairy farms. Open tanks are uncovered and commonly used for immediate cooling and storage of milk. Each tank type offers specific benefits and is chosen based on the dairy farm's requirements.</p></p>
