<p><p>AMR (anisotropic magnetoresistance) sensing ICs are devices used to detect and measure magnetic fields. The market for these ICs can be categorized into two types: Digital Output AMR Sensing ICs and Analog Output AMR Sensing ICs. The digital output type provides a binary signal indicating the presence or absence of a magnetic field. On the other hand, the analog output type produces a continuous voltage or current proportional to the strength of the magnetic field. These different types cater to various applications and customer requirements in the magnetic sensing industry.</p></p>
