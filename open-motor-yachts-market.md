<p><p>The Open Motor Yachts market includes two main types: Planing Hull and Displacement Hull. Planing Hull yachts are designed to achieve high speeds by lifting the hull out of the water as they move forward, reducing drag. These yachts are ideal for cruising and offer a thrilling experience but may sacrifice fuel efficiency and comfort at slower speeds. On the other hand, Displacement Hull yachts are built to travel through water, displacing it, at lower speeds. They provide better fuel efficiency, stability, and comfort, making them suitable for long-range cruising.</p></p>
