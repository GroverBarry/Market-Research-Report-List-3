<p><p>Electrostatic fieldmeters are devices used to measure the strength and distribution of electric fields. Non-contacting fieldmeters utilize proximity sensors to measure the field without physical contact with the object being measured. This type is widely used when measuring high-voltage equipment or in applications where contact may be unsafe or impractical. Contacting fieldmeters, on the other hand, require direct contact with the object being measured. This type is commonly used in applications where precise measurements and full control over the measurement are necessary, such as research laboratories or manufacturing environments.</p></p>
