<p><p>The industrial autoclave market is categorized into two types: vertical and horizontal autoclaves. Vertical industrial autoclaves are designed with a vertical chamber, ensuring efficient utilization of space. They are commonly used for small-scale applications and have advantages such as easy loading and unloading of materials. On the other hand, horizontal industrial autoclaves have a horizontal chamber and are suitable for larger-scale applications. They offer advantages like uniform distribution of heat and easier installation. However, selection of the type depends on the specific requirements and space availability of the industry.</p></p>
