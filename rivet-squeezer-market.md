<p><p>The Rivet Squeezer market offers three main types of rivet squeezers: pneumatic, hydraulic, and electric. Pneumatic rivet squeezers use compressed air to generate force, making them fast and powerful. Hydraulic rivet squeezers operate using pressurized fluid, providing precision and high force capabilities. Electric rivet squeezers utilize electric motors for forceful riveting, making them versatile and portable. These market types distinguish themselves through their power sources, resulting in different features and applications suited for various industries and tasks.</p></p>
