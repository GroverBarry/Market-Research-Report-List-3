Two popular types in this market are the worm gear screw jack and the ball screw jack. Worm gear screw jacks use a worm gear mechanism to convert rotational motion into linear motion, providing stable and reliable lifting capabilities. On the other hand, ball screw jacks utilize a ball screw mechanism, resulting in higher efficiency and precision. Both types are important components in industries requiring precise vertical or horizontal movements.</p></p>
