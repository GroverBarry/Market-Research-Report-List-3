<p><p>The commercial ceiling fan market can be categorized into two main types: AC ceiling fans and DC ceiling fans. On the other hand, DC ceiling fans use direct current and have gained popularity due to their energy efficiency and quiet operation. Although they are generally more expensive than AC ceiling fans, their long-term savings in energy costs make them a preferred choice for many commercial establishments.</p></p>
