<p><p>Neurovascular embolization coils are medical devices used in the treatment of vascular diseases in the brain or spinal cord. Neurovascular embolization coils help in preventing bleeding and reducing the risk of stroke or other severe complications.</p><p>The neurovascular embolization coils market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period.
