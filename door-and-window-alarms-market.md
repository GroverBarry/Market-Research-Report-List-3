<p><p>Door and window alarms are security devices designed to protect homes and businesses from unauthorized access. There are two main types of these alarms: wired and wireless. Wired alarms require physical connections between the sensors and control panel, making installation more complex and time-consuming. On the other hand, wireless alarms use radio frequency technology to transmit signals between the sensors and control panel, offering easier and quicker installation. Both types provide effective security solutions; however, wireless alarms are preferred by many due to their flexibility and ease of use.</p></p>
