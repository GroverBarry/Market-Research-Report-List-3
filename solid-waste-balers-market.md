<p><p>The solid waste balers market includes two main types: vertical balers and horizontal balers. Vertical balers are designed to compress waste materials vertically, making them ideal for spaces with limited floor area. They are commonly used for recycling cardboard, paper, and plastics. On the other hand, horizontal balers compress waste materials horizontally and are better suited for larger volumes of waste. They are often used in industries such as manufacturing, retail, and waste management. Both types of balers aim to optimize waste management and improve recycling processes.</p></p>
