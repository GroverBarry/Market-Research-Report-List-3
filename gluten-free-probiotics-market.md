<p><p>Gluten-free probiotics are dietary supplements or food products that contain beneficial bacteria strains and are specially formulated to be free of gluten. Therefore, gluten-free probiotics are specifically designed to cater to the needs of this growing consumer segment.</p><p>The global market for gluten-free probiotics has been experiencing significant growth in recent years. The increasing prevalence of celiac disease and gluten sensitivity, coupled with the rising awareness of the importance of gut health, has been driving the demand for gluten-free probiotics. Additionally, the growing focus on preventive healthcare and the rising popularity of functional foods and dietary supplements have further fueled market growth.</p><p>Apart from addressing digestive health issues, gluten-free probiotics are also gaining traction for their potential benefits in immunity enhancement, weight management, and mental health improvement.
