The market for smart water softeners can be divided into two main types: salt-based water softeners and salt-free water softeners. Salt-based water softeners use a process called ion exchange to remove minerals that contribute to hardness in water. On the other hand, salt-free water softeners use alternative methods like template-assisted crystallization to reduce hardness without adding sodium to the water. Both types of smart water softeners offer solutions for addressing water hardness, catering to different preferences and requirements.</p></p>
