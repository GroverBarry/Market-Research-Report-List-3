<p><p>Mineral wool composite market consists of two main types - Rock wool composite and Glass wool composite. Rock wool composite is made from natural rock minerals such as basalt and dolomite, while glass wool composite is made from recycled glass. Both materials offer excellent thermal insulation, fire resistance, and soundproofing properties. Rock wool composite is known for higher durability and temperature resistance, while glass wool composite is preferred for its lightweight and flexible nature. These materials are widely used in construction, automotive, and industrial applications.</p></p>
