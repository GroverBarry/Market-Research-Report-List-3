<p><p>The counter drone market consists of three main types: detection, disruption, and detection & disruption. Detection involves the use of sensors, radars, and cameras to identify and track unauthorized drones. Disruption employs technologies like radio frequency jammers or laser systems to interfere with the drone's navigation or communication. Detection & disruption combines both approaches by first identifying the drone and then initiating measures to neutralize its threat. These market types cater to the increasing need for protecting critical infrastructures, public spaces, and individuals from potential security breaches by unauthorized drones.</p></p>
