<p><p>Laser plastic welding machines are categorized into different types based on the laser source used for the welding process. Diode laser welding machines utilize diodes as the laser source, offering moderate power output and good beam quality. Fiber laser welding machines use optical fibers to deliver the laser beam, allowing for high power output and excellent beam quality. Other types of laser plastic welding machines may include CO2 lasers or Nd:YAG lasers. Each type of machine has its own advantages and suitability for specific applications in the plastic welding industry.</p></p>
