<p><p>Snap gage is a measuring device used in the manufacturing industry to assess the dimensional conformity of parts, mostly metal components. It is a simple and efficient tool that consists of go and no-go gages, which are designed to determine if a part is within tolerance limits or not. This process can be performed quickly by placing the part between the gages and checking for proper fit.</p><p>The global snap gage market is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years. Outer diameter caliper snap gages, on the other hand, are used to measure the outside diameter of cylindrical objects, ensuring they are within the required range. Both types of snap gages play a crucial role in quality control and precision measurement processes, helping to guarantee accurate dimensions and maintain product integrity.</p></p>
