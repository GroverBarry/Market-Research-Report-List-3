<p><p>Elemental analyzers are instruments used to determine the elemental composition of a sample. They are classified into three types: manual, semi-automatic, and fully automatic. A manual elemental analyzer requires human intervention for each step of the analysis process, including sample preparation and measurement. Semi-automatic analyzers automate some steps, such as sample introduction and data acquisition, reducing human involvement but still requiring occasional intervention. Fully automatic analyzers are highly automated, handling all steps of the analysis process without human intervention. These different types cater to varying levels of user involvement and efficiency in elemental analysis.</p></p>
