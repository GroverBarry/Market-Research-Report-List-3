<p><p>Hot-dip galvanized steel wire market consists of two main types: pure zinc and zinc alloy. Pure zinc refers to steel wire that is coated with a layer of 100% zinc. This type of coating offers excellent corrosion resistance and is commonly used in applications where protection against rust is necessary. Zinc alloy, on the other hand, is a mix of zinc and other elements such as aluminum or magnesium. This alloy coating provides improved corrosion resistance, enhanced aesthetics, and better performance in certain environments compared to pure zinc coatings.</p></p>
