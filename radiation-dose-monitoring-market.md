<p><ul><li>Dosimeters</li><li>Area Process Monitors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Radiation Dose Monitoring Market includes two major types: dosimeters and area process monitors. Dosimeters are personal devices worn by individuals working in radiation-prone environments to track their exposure levels. They provide real-time data on radiation doses accumulated by the wearer. On the other hand, area process monitors are used to measure and monitor radiation levels in specific areas, such as laboratories or industrial facilities. These devices ensure the safety of workers and help prevent potential hazards by alerting them when radiation levels exceed the acceptable limit.</p></p>
