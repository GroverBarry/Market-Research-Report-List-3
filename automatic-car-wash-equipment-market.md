<p><p>The automatic car wash equipment market consists of two major types: gantry car wash and conveyor tunnel system. Gantry car wash uses a stationary frame that moves horizontally while the vehicle remains stationary. It typically employs high-pressure water jets, brushes, and detergents to clean the vehicle. Similarly, the conveyor tunnel system moves the vehicle through a series of stations, including pre-wash, foam application, brushing, rinsing, and drying. Both types aim to provide efficient and automated car cleaning services, catering to the growing demand for convenience and time-saving options in the automotive industry.</p></p>
