<p><p>The Industrial Forming Press market can be classified into three types based on their operation mode: fully-automatic, semi-automatic, and manual. Fully-automatic forming presses are designed to autonomously execute the entire forming process, requiring no human intervention. Semi-automatic presses require operators to load and unload materials, while the forming process is automated. Manual forming presses rely entirely on human operation, with operators controlling every aspect of the forming process. These different types cater to diverse industrial needs, offering varying levels of automation and human involvement in forming operations.</p></p>
