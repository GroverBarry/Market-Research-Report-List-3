<p><p>The Medical Lateral Flow Assay (LFA) Testing Services Market includes different types of tests such as Sandwich Assays and Competitive Assays. Sandwich Assays involve capturing the target molecule between two antibodies, providing a high level of sensitivity and specificity. Competitive Assays, on the other hand, involve the competition between the target molecule and a labeled antigen for binding to a limited number of binding sites, allowing for quantitative analysis. Both types of assays are widely used in the medical industry for diagnostic purposes.</p></p>
