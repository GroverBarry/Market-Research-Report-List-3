<p><p>Data Center Monitoring Systems (DCMS) are software solutions that enable the real-time monitoring and management of data centers. The market for DCMS can be classified into two main types: cloud-based and internal deployment. Cloud-based DCMS refers to systems that are hosted and managed by third-party providers, allowing users to access the software remotely via the internet. On the other hand, internal deployment DCMS involves installing and managing the software directly on the organization's own servers or infrastructure. Both types offer monitoring capabilities but differ in terms of hosting and management.</p></p>
