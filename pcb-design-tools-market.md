<p><p>PCB design tools are software applications used by electronic engineers and designers to create and design printed circuit boards (PCBs). The increasing demand for advanced and miniaturized electronic devices, coupled with the rapidly evolving Internet of Things (IoT) ecosystem, has been a significant driver for the market. Furthermore, the integration of 3D modeling and simulation capabilities into PCB design tools has been gaining traction, allowing designers to simulate and analyze the performance of the PCB before manufacturing.</p><p>In terms of latest trends, cloud-based PCB design tools are gaining popularity due to their flexibility, scalability, and cost-effectiveness.
