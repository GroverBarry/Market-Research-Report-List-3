<p><p>Underwater pelletizing system is a technology used in the plastic industry to convert molten plastics into solid pellets. This process involves passing the molten plastic through a die plate and cutting it into pellets while still submerged in water. The pellets are then dried and transported for further processing or packaging.</p><p>The underwater pelletizing system offers numerous advantages over traditional pelletizing methods, such as reduced energy consumption, improved pellet quality, and increased production efficiency. This technology is widely used in various end-use industries, including automotive, packaging, healthcare, and consumer goods.</p><p>The underwater pelletizing system market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period.
