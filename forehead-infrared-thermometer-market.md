<p><p>The Forehead Infrared Thermometer market consists of two main types: non-contact type and contact type. Non-contact type thermometers function by measuring the infrared radiation emitted from the forehead without making physical contact. This type of thermometer is more suitable for quick and hygienic use, making it popular in public places and medical settings. In contrast, contact type thermometers require direct physical contact with the forehead to measure body temperature. These thermometers are often used in households and clinics where accuracy and consistency are prioritized.</p></p>
