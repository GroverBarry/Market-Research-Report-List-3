<p><p>The Industrial Air Source Heat Pump market includes two main types: Air-To-Air Heat Pump and Air-To-Water Heat Pump. Air-To-Air Heat Pump technology transfers heat from the outdoor air into indoor spaces, providing heating and cooling. This type is commonly used in residential and commercial buildings. On the other hand, Air-To-Water Heat Pump technology transfers heat from the outdoor air to a water-based central heating system. It is often used in industrial applications, like large factories or warehouses, to provide heat for space heating or hot water production.</p></p>
