Therefore, water hardness removal is essential to improve water quality for various applications.</p><p>The market for water hardness removal is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period. <p><p>The water hardness removal market is classified into three main types: salt-based water hardness removal, salt-free water hardness removal, and others. Salt-based water hardness removal systems use ion exchange technology to remove minerals like calcium and magnesium from water, often utilizing salt to regenerate the resin bed. Salt-free water hardness removal systems, also known as descalers, use different methods like electromagnetic waves or template-assisted crystallization to prevent minerals from adhering to surfaces. The "others" category includes alternative technologies or devices that may offer different approaches to tackle the issue of water hardness, such as electronic water conditioners or magnetic systems.</p></p>
