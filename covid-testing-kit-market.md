<p><p>The Covid Testing Kit market consists of two main types of tests: Nucleic Acid Testing and Antibody Testing. Nucleic Acid Testing involves the detection of the virus's genetic material to confirm an active infection. It is typically conducted through a nasal or throat swab. On the other hand, Antibody Testing detects the presence of antibodies produced by the immune system in response to the infection. It is conducted through a blood sample. Both types of tests play a crucial role in identifying and monitoring Covid-19 cases, helping to control the spread of the virus.</p></p>
