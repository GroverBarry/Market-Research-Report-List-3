Monocular microscopes have a single eyepiece, while binocular microscopes have two eyepieces, allowing for more comfortable and natural viewing. Trinocular microscopes, on the other hand, have an additional port for attaching a camera or other imaging devices for capturing images or recording videos. Each type offers unique advantages and suitability based on users' preferences and educational requirements.</p></p>
