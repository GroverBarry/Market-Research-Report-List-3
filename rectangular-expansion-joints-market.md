<p><p>Rectangular expansion joints are used to accommodate thermal expansion and contraction in structures. Fillet type expansion joints have rounded corners and are ideal for applications where flow direction changes occur. Single oblique angle expansion joints have a diagonal corner, providing flexibility in a single direction. Camera angle expansion joints have two diagonal corners, offering flexibility in two directions. Each of these market types addresses specific needs, offering customized solutions for various applications in industries such as construction, automotive, and aerospace.</p></p>
