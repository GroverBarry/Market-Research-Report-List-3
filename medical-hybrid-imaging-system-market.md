<p><p>Medical hybrid imaging systems combine two or more imaging technologies to provide a comprehensive view of the patient's anatomy and function. Medical hybrid imaging systems provide enhanced anatomical and functional information, leading to improved disease detection and monitoring. Furthermore, the use of hybrid imaging reduces the need for multiple diagnostic tests, lowering healthcare costs and improving efficiency.</p><p>One of the latest trends in the medical hybrid imaging system market is the integration of artificial intelligence (AI). AI algorithms can analyze large volumes of imaging data quickly and accurately, assisting radiologists in diagnosing diseases and predicting treatment response.
