<p><p>The side sealer market is divided into two main types: Automatic and Manual. Automatic side sealers are machines that automatically seal and wrap packages without the need for human intervention. They are efficient and ideal for high-volume production environments. On the other hand, Manual side sealers require manual operation, where an operator feeds the package into the machine and manually activates the sealing process. They are suitable for smaller operations or when a lower volume of packaging is required. Both types serve the purpose of securely sealing packages, but with different levels of automation and output capacity.</p></p>
