<p><strong>The Smart Sprinkler Controller Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Weather-Based Controllers</li><li>Sensor-Based Controllers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Weather-based controllers are a type of smart sprinkler controller that adjusts irrigation schedules based on local weather conditions. These controllers use real-time weather data to determine the optimal watering schedule, taking into account factors such as rainfall and evaporation rates. On the other hand, sensor-based controllers rely on sensors in the soil or plants to determine when watering is needed. These sensors measure moisture levels and other environmental factors to ensure that irrigation is only applied when necessary, leading to water conservation and improved plant health.</p></p>
