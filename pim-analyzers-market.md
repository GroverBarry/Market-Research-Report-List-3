<p><p>PIM Analyzers, or Passive Intermodulation Analyzers, are electronic test instruments used to measure and analyze the presence of passive intermodulation distortions in communication systems. PIM Analyzers help in locating and resolving these issues, ensuring efficient and reliable communication.</p><p>The market for PIM Analyzers is expected to witness significant growth in the forecast period.
