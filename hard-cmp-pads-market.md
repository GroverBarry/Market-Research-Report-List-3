<p><strong>The Hard CMP Pads Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Abrasive Type</li><li>Normal Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hard CMP (Chemical Mechanical Polishing) pads are primarily used in semiconductor manufacturing for planarizing and polishing the surface of wafers. There are two main types of Hard CMP pads: Abrasive Type and Normal Type. </p><p>Abrasive Type pads contain a higher concentration of abrasive particles, typically made of cerium oxide or aluminum oxide. These pads provide enhanced material removal capabilities, making them suitable for polishing harder materials such as silicon dioxide or tungsten.</p><p>Normal Type pads have a lower concentration of abrasive particles and are used for polishing softer materials like copper or aluminum. These pads deliver a smoother surface finish and minimize damage to the wafer during the polishing process.</p></p>
