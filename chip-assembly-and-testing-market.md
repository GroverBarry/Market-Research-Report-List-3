<p><p>Chip Assembly and Testing market includes three main segments: Wafer Probing, Final Test, and Other. </p><p>Wafer Probing involves testing individual dies on the wafer base. The dies are electrically tested to detect any defects or malfunctions before they are packaged.</p><p>Final Test is the last stage of chip manufacturing, where the packaged dies are tested for functionality, performance, and reliability. This step assures the quality of the final packaged chips before they are sent to customers.</p><p>The Other market segment includes various services, such as assembly and testing equipment, materials, and software solutions that support chip manufacturing and testing processes. These products are essential for ensuring the smooth assembly and testing operations.</p></p>
