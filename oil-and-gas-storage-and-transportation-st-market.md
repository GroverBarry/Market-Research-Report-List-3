<p><p>The oil and gas storage and transportation (S&T) market involves various types of fuels and technologies. PNG (piped natural gas), LNG (liquefied natural gas), CNG (compressed natural gas), ANG (adsorbed natural gas), and NGH (natural gas hydrates) are some common types. PNG is natural gas supplied through pipelines. LNG refers to natural gas converted into a liquid state for transportation and storage. CNG is natural gas compressed to a high pressure for ease of transportation. ANG involves natural gas stored under lower pressure using adsorbent materials. NGH represents methane gas trapped within ice-like structures. Other types may include synthetic fuels or alternative energy sources.</p></p>
