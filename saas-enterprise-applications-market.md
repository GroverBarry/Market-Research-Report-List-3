<p><p>On-premise enterprise applications refer to software that is installed and hosted on local servers within a company's infrastructure. These applications require maintenance, updates, and support from the organization's IT team. On the other hand, cloud-based enterprise applications are hosted and accessed over the internet. These applications are managed by the service provider, offering scalability, flexibility, and reduced maintenance and infrastructure costs.
