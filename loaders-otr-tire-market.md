<p><p>Loaders OTR tire market consists of two main types: radial tires and bias tires. Radial tires are constructed with steel belts running perpendicular to the direction of travel, providing higher flexibility and better traction. They offer improved stability and longer tread life. On the other hand, bias tires use a crisscross ply construction that provides better puncture resistance and durability in rough terrains. Each type has its own advantages and is suitable for different applications in the loaders OTR tire market.</p></p>
