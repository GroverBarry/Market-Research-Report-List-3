<p><p>Submerged Arc Welding (SAW) is a welding process that uses a consumable electrode and a blanket of granular flux to create an electric arc between the electrode and the workpiece. The arc melts the electrode and the workpiece, while the flux shields the molten metal from atmospheric contamination and provides a protective layer. SAW is commonly used in heavy fabrication and manufacturing industries for applications where high-quality, efficient welding is required, such as in shipbuilding, construction, and pipe welding.</p><p>The Submerged Arc Welding Market is expected to experience a significant growth rate during the forecast period.
