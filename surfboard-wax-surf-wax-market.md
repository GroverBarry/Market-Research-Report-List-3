The market for surfboard wax consists of two main types: base coat wax and top coat wax. Base coat wax is used as the initial layer on the surfboard, helping to ensure that the top coat wax adheres properly. Top coat wax is then applied on top of the base coat to provide better traction and grip. Both base coat and top coat wax play crucial roles in enhancing surfers' performance and safety in the water.</p></p>
