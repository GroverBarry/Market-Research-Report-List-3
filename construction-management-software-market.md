<p><p>The construction management software market comprises two types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based construction management software is accessed and operated through the internet, offering flexibility, scalability, and reduced IT costs by eliminating the need for physical servers on-site. On the other hand, on-premises construction management software is installed and maintained on local servers within the organization's premises. This type provides full control and security but requires significant upfront investment and ongoing IT maintenance. Both options have their advantages and organizations choose based on their specific needs and preferences.</p></p>
