<p><p>An air conditioning compressor clutch is an important component of an air conditioning system in automotive vehicles. It is responsible for engaging and disengaging the compressor, which is crucial for controlling the cooling process. When the clutch is engaged, it connects the compressor to the engine's rotating shaft, allowing it to compress and circulate the refrigerant to cool the air inside the vehicle. When not in use, the clutch disconnects the compressor, preventing unnecessary wear and tear and reducing power consumption.</p><p>The global air conditioning compressor clutch market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period.
