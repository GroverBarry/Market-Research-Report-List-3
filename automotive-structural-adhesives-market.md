<p><ul><li>Epoxy</li><li>Polyurethane</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive structural adhesives are used to bond different materials in vehicle manufacturing. Epoxy adhesives are known for their strong bonding abilities and resistance to chemicals and heat, making them ideal for structural applications. Polyurethane adhesives offer flexibility and durability, making them suitable for applications requiring impact resistance and vibration dampening. Both types of adhesives have their unique advantages and are commonly used in the automotive industry for joining dissimilar materials such as metals and composites to improve overall vehicle strength and performance.</p></p>
