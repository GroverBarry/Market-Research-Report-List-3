<p><p>Evacuation slides are crucial safety devices found on aircraft to ensure a quick and safe exit during emergencies. There are two main types of evacuation slides: single-lane type and dual-lane type. In the single-lane type, there is only one chute, allowing passengers to slide down one at a time. On the other hand, the dual-lane type features two parallel chutes, enabling more people to evacuate simultaneously. These market types cater to different aircraft sizes, passenger capacities, evacuation requirements, and preferences of airline carriers and manufacturers.</p></p>
