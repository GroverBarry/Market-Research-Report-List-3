<p><p>The industrial silica market encompasses two main types: crystalline state and amorphous state. Crystalline state silica refers to a highly pure form of silica with a well-defined crystal structure. It is widely used in various applications, including glass manufacturing, electronics, and construction materials. On the other hand, amorphous state silica does not have a specific crystal structure and is often found in a powder or gel-like form. It is utilized in diverse industries such as paints, coatings, and personal care products due to its high surface area and absorbent properties.</p></p>
