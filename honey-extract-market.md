<p><p>The honey extract market consists of two main types: powder and liquid. Powdered honey extract is made by spray drying liquid honey to remove all the moisture, resulting in a fine, dry powder. It is convenient to use and has a longer shelf life. Liquid honey extract, on the other hand, is the natural form of honey that hasn't been dehydrated. It retains its original consistency and flavor profile. Both types of honey extract are widely used in various industries such as food, beverages, cosmetics, and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
