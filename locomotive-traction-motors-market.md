<p><p>The locomotive traction motors market consists of two main types: induction traction motor and permanent-magnet synchronous traction motor. Induction traction motor relies on electromagnetic induction to provide power to the locomotive. On the other hand, permanent-magnet synchronous traction motor utilizes neodymium magnets to generate the required magnetic field. Both types play a crucial role in powering locomotives, but use different mechanisms to achieve the same goal of converting electrical energy into mechanical motion for efficient train operation.</p></p>
