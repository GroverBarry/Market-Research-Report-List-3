<p><p>Non-woven abrasive locking discs are available in two main types: Quick Change Type and Arbor Hole Type. Quick Change Type discs have a convenient twist-on design that allows for easy and fast disc changes without the need for tools. On the other hand, Arbor Hole Type discs require a backing pad with a matching arbor hole for attachment. Both types provide efficient and effective grinding, finishing, and polishing solutions for various applications in the metalworking, woodworking, and automotive industries.</p></p>
