<p><p>Urinalysis market is divided into two main types - consumables and instruments. Consumables refer to the disposable items used in the urinalysis tests, such as reagent strips, urine cups, and collection tubes. These items are essential for conducting the tests and are meant to be thrown away after a single-use. On the other hand, instruments include the machines and devices used for analyzing the urine samples, such as analyzers and microscopy systems. These instruments provide accurate measurements and analysis of various components in the urine, aiding in diagnosis and monitoring of health conditions.</p></p>
