<p><ul><li>Shaking</li><li>Cutting</li><li>Dragging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automatic fruit picking machines are categorized into three types - shaking, cutting, and dragging. Shaking machines use vibration or mechanical shaking to dislodge ripe fruits from the tree branches, allowing them to fall into a collection container. Cutting machines, as the name suggests, use blades or tools to cut the fruit from the tree. Dragging machines are equipped with brushes or other mechanisms that pull the fruits from the branches. These different types of machines offer efficient and automated strategies for harvesting fruits, reducing manual labor and increasing productivity in the agricultural industry.</p></p>
