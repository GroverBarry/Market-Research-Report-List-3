<p><strong>The Automatic Bilge Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Float Switch</li><li>No Float Switch</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automatic Bilge Pump market includes two main types: Float Switch and No Float Switch. The Float Switch type pumps are equipped with a switch that automatically activates the pump when the water level rises above a certain point. This ensures continuous pumping without human intervention. On the other hand, the No Float Switch pumps do not have a switch mechanism and require manual activation. These pumps are suitable for smaller water bodies where the water level fluctuations are not significant and do not require constant monitoring.</p></p>
