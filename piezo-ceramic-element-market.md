<p><p>The Piezo Ceramic Element market can be categorized into two types: Direct Radiation Type and Indirect Radiation Type. Direct Radiation Type refers to piezo ceramic elements that emit sound directly into the surrounding air when stimulated by an electric signal. On the other hand, Indirect Radiation Type refers to piezo ceramic elements that convert the electrical signal into mechanical vibrations, which are then transferred to a diaphragm or other medium to generate sound. Both types have their advantages and are used in various applications according to specific requirements.</p></p>
