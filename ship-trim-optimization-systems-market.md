<p><p>Ship trim optimization systems can be classified into two types, namely modular optimization and holistic optimization. Modular optimization focuses on specific aspects of ship trim, such as fuel consumption, stability, and maneuverability, and employs separate systems to optimize each element individually. On the other hand, holistic optimization takes a comprehensive approach and considers all aspects of ship trim simultaneously to achieve overall efficiency and performance. While modular optimization allows for targeted improvements, holistic optimization ensures a synergistic and integrated solution for maximizing ship trim efficiency.</p></p>
