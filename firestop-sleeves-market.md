<p><p>The Firestop Sleeves market consists of two types: Pouring and Embedded. Pouring Firestop Sleeves are designed to be installed by pouring a fire-resistant material around the sleeves, creating a barrier against fire and smoke. Embedded Firestop Sleeves, on the other hand, are installed during the construction process by embedding them into the walls or floors, ensuring that they are securely in place. Both types of Firestop Sleeves serve the purpose of preventing the spread of fire and smoke through openings in buildings, providing increased safety.</p></p>
