<p><p>The multimode fiber splitter market offers various types of splitters, including 1×2, 1×4, 1×8, 2×2, and others. In a 1×2 splitter, one input signal can be divided into two output signals. Similarly, a 1×4 splitter splits one input into four outputs, a 1×8 splitter into eight outputs, and a 2×2 splitter allows two input signals to be divided into two outputs each. The "others" category refers to splitters with different configurations or specifications. These different types cater to various networking and communication needs, providing flexibility and efficiency in signal distribution.</p></p>
