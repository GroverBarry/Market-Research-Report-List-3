<p><p>Cerebral infarction therapy market includes different types of infarcts. Partial Anterior Circulation Infarct (PACI) refers to restricted blood flow in certain brain areas, leading to specific symptoms. Total Anterior Circulation Infarct (TACI) occurs when blood supply to most of the anterior brain circulation is disrupted, resulting in various severe symptoms. Posterior Circulation Infarct (POCI) happens when blood flow is affected in the posterior part of the brain, causing specific neurological issues. These different types of infarcts require specific therapeutic approaches for effective treatment and management.</p></p>
