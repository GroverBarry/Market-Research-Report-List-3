<p><p>The laser cleaning head market consists of two main types: Continuous Wave (CW) and Pulse. Continuous Wave lasers emit a continuous, steady beam of laser energy, providing a constant cleaning effect. These lasers are commonly used for applications that require continuous and precise cleaning, such as in industrial manufacturing processes. On the other hand, Pulse lasers emit short bursts of high-energy laser beams, creating a rapid cleaning effect. Pulse lasers are often used for applications that require a strong and fast cleaning action, such as in the removal of stubborn contaminants or coatings.</p></p>
