<p><p>The industrial gears market can be classified into various types based on their design and applications. Spur gears are the most common type, consisting of straight teeth parallel to the gear axis. Helical gears have angled teeth, offering smooth and quiet operation. Gear racks are used to convert rotary motion into linear motion. Worm gears are designed with a screw-like shaft and are known for their high gear ratio, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications. Bevel gears have teeth that are angled, allowing transmission between non-parallel shafts. Other types include planetary gears, hypoid gears, and spiral bevel gears.</p></p>
