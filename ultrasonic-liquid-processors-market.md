<p><p>Ultrasonic liquid processors, also known as ultrasonic homogenizers or sonicators, are devices that use high-frequency sound waves to disrupt particles in a liquid medium. This disruption, known as ultrasonic cavitation, leads to various effects such as emulsification, dispersing, and cell disruption. These processors are widely used in industries such as pharmaceuticals, biotechnology, food and beverage, paints and coatings, and others.</p><p>The ultrasonic liquid processors market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period.
