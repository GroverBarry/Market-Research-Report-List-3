<p><p>The bottling equipment market can be categorized into three types: automatic, semi-automatic, and manual. Automatic bottling equipment refers to machines that perform all the bottling processes automatically without requiring much human intervention. Semi-automatic bottling equipment partially automates the bottling process, with some tasks still requiring human intervention. Manual bottling equipment involves a more hands-on approach, requiring operators to perform the majority of the bottling processes manually. Each type offers different levels of efficiency and productivity, allowing businesses to choose the equipment that best suits their needs in terms of output and labor requirements.</p></p>
