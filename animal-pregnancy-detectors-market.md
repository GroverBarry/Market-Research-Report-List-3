<p><p>Animal pregnancy detectors are devices used for determining the pregnancy status of various animals. One type of pregnancy detector is ultrasonic wave-based. These detectors utilize high-frequency sound waves to produce images of the uterus, allowing veterinarians and breeders to visualize the fetus and confirm pregnancy. Other market types include hormone-based detectors that analyze hormonal changes in the animal's body as a result of pregnancy. These detectors measure specific hormones levels in bodily fluids or urine, indicating the presence of a pregnancy. Both ultrasound and hormone-based detectors play crucial roles in diagnosing animal pregnancy accurately.</p></p>
