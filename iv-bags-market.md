<p><p>The IV bags market comprises three main types: flex plastic IV bags, semi-rigid IV bags, and glass bottles. Flex plastic IV bags are made of flexible plastic materials, allowing for easy handling and transportation. Semi-rigid IV bags have a slightly firmer structure and are often used for larger volumes of fluids. Glass bottles are the traditional option for IV administration, offering superior stability but requiring more precautions due to the risk of breakage. Each type serves different purposes and is chosen based on factors like patient needs and healthcare provider preferences.</p></p>
