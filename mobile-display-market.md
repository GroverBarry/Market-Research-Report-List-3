<p><p>The mobile display market consists of three main types: LCD (Liquid Crystal Display) Displays, OLED (Organic Light Emitting Diode) Displays, and LED (Light Emitting Diode) Displays. LCD Displays use a backlight to illuminate the screen, resulting in good color accuracy but lower contrast. OLED Displays use self-emitting diodes to produce their own light, offering high contrast and vibrant colors. LED Displays use an array of tiny LEDs to create the image, providing high brightness and energy efficiency. Each type has its own advantages and is used in various mobile devices based on specific requirements.</p></p>
