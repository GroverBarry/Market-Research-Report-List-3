<p><p>The personal safety alarms market can be divided into two types: active alarms and passive alarms. Active alarms require the user to activate them in case of an emergency by pressing a button or pulling a pin. They are designed to alert others nearby about the dangerous situation. On the other hand, passive alarms are triggered automatically without any action required from the user. These alarms are usually equipped with sensors that detect unusual movements, sounds, or sudden impacts, and then emit a loud sound or signal for help.</p></p>
