<p><strong>The Night-vision Goggles Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Image Intensifier</li><li>Thermal Image</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Night vision goggles come in two main types- Image Intensifier and Thermal Image. Image Intensifier goggles work by amplifying existing light, making objects visible in low-light situations. They are commonly used in military and law enforcement applications. On the other hand, Thermal Image goggles detect the heat emitted by objects, allowing users to see in total darkness. They are often used in surveillance, hunting, and search and rescue operations. Both types cater to different needs and are utilized in various industries for enhanced visibility in the dark.</p></p>
