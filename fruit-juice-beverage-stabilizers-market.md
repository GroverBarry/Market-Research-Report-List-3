<p><p>The fruit juice beverage stabilizers market is segmented into different types. Xanthan Gum, Carrageenan, Gum Arabic, Carboxymethyl Cellulose (CMC), and Others are the key types of stabilizers used in this market. Xanthan Gum is a polysaccharide that helps to retain the texture and consistency of fruit juice. Carrageenan, derived from red seaweed, acts as a thickening and stabilizing agent. Gum Arabic is a natural gum that provides stability and viscosity to fruit juices. Carboxymethyl Cellulose (CMC) is a cellulose derivative that enhances the texture and suspension of particles in beverages. Other stabilizers may include pectin, alginate, and modified starches.</p></p>
