<p><p>The vertical CNC drilling machine market refers to the segment of the industry that focuses on machines that have a vertical orientation, meaning the drill is in a perpendicular position to the workpiece. These machines are often used for drilling holes in materials such as metal, wood, or plastic. On the other hand, the horizontal CNC drilling machine market involves machines with a horizontal orientation, where the drill is parallel to the workpiece. These machines are suitable for drilling multiple holes in a row or for larger workpieces.</p></p>
