<p><p>Industrial energy storage systems can be categorized into three types: electrochemical energy storage systems, mechanical energy storage systems, and thermal storage. </p><p>Electrochemical energy storage systems involve the conversion of chemical energy into electrical energy and are commonly used in applications such as batteries and fuel cells. Mechanical energy storage systems store energy in the form of mechanical potential energy and release it when needed, such as in flywheels or compressed air energy storage. Thermal storage systems store energy in the form of heat and utilize it for various industrial processes or electricity generation.</p></p>
