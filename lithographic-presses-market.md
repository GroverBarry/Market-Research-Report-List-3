<p><p>Lithographic presses are printing machines that use lithography, a printing method based on the principle of oil and water repelling each other, to produce high-quality prints and images. Lithographic printing is widely used in the commercial printing industry for various applications such as newspapers, magazines, packaging materials, and labels.</p><p>The current outlook of the lithographic presses market is promising.
