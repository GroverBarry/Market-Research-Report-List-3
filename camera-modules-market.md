<p><p>The camera modules market comprises various types of components, including image sensors, lens, flexible printed circuit (FPC), digital signal processor (DSP), and others. Image sensors play a crucial role in capturing images by converting light into electrical signals. The lens focuses the light onto the image sensor, ensuring high-quality imaging. FPC, or flexible circuitry, provides connectivity between different components within the camera module. DSP processes the digital signals from the image sensor and enhances image quality. Other components may include autofocus mechanisms, image stabilization technology, and infrared filters, among others.</p></p>
