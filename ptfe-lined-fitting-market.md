<p><p>The PTFE lined fitting market consists of various types of fittings that are lined with PTFE (polytetrafluoroethylene) material. PTFE Lined Tees enable branching of pipelines. PTFE Lined Flanges are used for joining pipes and fittings. PTFE Lined Elbows help change the direction of flow in a pipeline. PTFE Lined Concentric Reducers are fittings that reduce the pipe size while maintaining alignment. Lastly, there are other types of fittings available in the market that cater to specific needs of the industry.</p></p>
