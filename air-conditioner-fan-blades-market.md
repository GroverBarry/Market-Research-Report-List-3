<p><p>The Air Conditioner Fan Blades Market consists of two main types: Cross Flow Fan Blades and Centrifugal Fan Blades. </p><p>Cross Flow Fan Blades are designed in a way that the air flows parallel to the blade axis, resulting in a wider range of air distribution. They are commonly used in small to medium-sized air conditioning units or refrigeration systems. </p><p>On the other hand, Centrifugal Fan Blades have a curved shape, allowing the air to flow perpendicular to the blade axis. They are known for their high air pressure and are typically used in larger air conditioning systems or industrial applications.</p></p>
