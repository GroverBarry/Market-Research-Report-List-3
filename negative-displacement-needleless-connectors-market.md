<p><p>Negative displacement needleless connectors are medical devices used to provide a secure and contamination-free method for connecting various medical devices, such as IV tubing, to patients. These connectors are available in two main types: disposable and reusable. Disposable connectors are designed for single-use and are discarded after each patient interaction to minimize the risk of cross-contamination and infection. Reusable connectors, on the other hand, can be sterilized and used multiple times, making them a cost-effective option for healthcare facilities. Both types of connectors play a crucial role in ensuring patient safety and preventing healthcare-associated infections.</p></p>
