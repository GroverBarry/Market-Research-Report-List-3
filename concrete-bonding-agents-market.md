<p><ul><li>Cementitious latex based</li><li>Epoxy based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Concrete bonding agents are used to improve the adhesion between old and new concrete surfaces. Cementitious latex-based bonding agents are composed of a blend of cement, polymers, and additives. They are commonly used for bonding new concrete to existing concrete. On the other hand, epoxy-based bonding agents contain epoxy resins that provide a strong bond between concrete surfaces. These agents are particularly useful in applications where high bond strength is required, such as in industrial settings or for repairing concrete structures.</p></p>
