<p><p>The rod mill market is divided into two types - wet grinding type and dry grinding type. In the wet grinding type, the material is fed through the barrel and is mixed with water during the grinding process, resulting in a slurry. This type is generally used for mineral processing and requires higher energy consumption. Conversely, the dry grinding type does not involve water and operates without a slurry, making it suitable for materials with low moisture content and requiring lower energy consumption.</p></p>
