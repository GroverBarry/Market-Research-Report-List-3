<p><strong>The Automotive Rear-View Camera Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Exterior Mirrors</li><li>Interior Mirrors</li><li>Under Rearview Mirrors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive Rear-View Camera Market has three main types based on their placement in vehicles. Exterior Mirrors house cameras on the sides of the vehicle, providing a view of blind spots. Interior Mirrors have built-in cameras to show the rear view on a screen in the mirror. Under Rearview Mirrors are installed under the rear bumper, giving a view of obstacles when parking or reversing. These different types cater to various needs and preferences of drivers for safer and more convenient driving experiences.</p></p>
