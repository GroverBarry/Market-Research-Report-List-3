<p><p>Rangefinder Market Types:</p><p>1. Photoelectric Rangefinder: A device that calculates the distance to a target by emitting a beam of light and measuring the time it takes for the light to bounce back. It is commonly used in surveying, hunting, and photography applications.</p><p>2. Acoustic Rangefinder: A system that determines the distance of an object by emitting sound waves and measuring the time they take to return. This technology is widely used in underwater applications, such as navigation, mapping, and fish finding. Both photoelectric and acoustic rangefinders provide accurate distance measurements for various purposes.</p></p>
