There are two main types of variable capacity pumps: variable displacement piston pumps and variable displacement vane pumps. Variable displacement piston pumps use pistons to vary the volume of fluid being pumped, while variable displacement vane pumps use vanes to achieve the same effect. Both types of pumps are commonly used in industries where fluid flow needs to be regulated accurately and efficiently.</p></p>
