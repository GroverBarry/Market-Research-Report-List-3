<p><p>The moulding maize starch market encompasses three types of starch: rapidly digestible starch, slowly digestible starch, and resistant starch. Rapidly digestible starch is broken down quickly by the body, providing fast energy release. Slowly digestible starch is broken down at a slower rate, leading to a gradual energy release. Resistant starch, on the other hand, resists digestion and acts as a source of dietary fiber, benefiting gut health. These different starch types cater to various consumer needs and preferences in terms of energy levels and digestion.</p></p>
