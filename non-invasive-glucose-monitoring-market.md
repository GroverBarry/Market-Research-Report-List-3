<p><p>The non-invasive glucose monitoring market comprises two types of products - wearable blood glucose monitoring systems and table top blood glucose monitoring systems. Wearable blood glucose monitoring systems are devices that can be attached to the body and provide continuous glucose monitoring without the need for frequent invasive blood sampling. Table top blood glucose monitoring systems, on the other hand, are devices that are placed on a surface and used to measure blood glucose levels using a small drop of blood obtained through a finger prick test.</p></p>
