<p><p>The Anti Submarine Rocket Launch System (ASRLS) market includes three main types: Hot Launch, Cold Launch, and Concentric Canister Launch. Hot Launch involves firing the rocket directly from the launch tube, utilizing a high-pressure gas system. Cold Launch utilizes a gas generator to eject the rocket from the launch tube before the rocket's engine ignites. Concentric Canister Launch involves placing the rocket in a sealed canister and using gas pressure to eject it from the launch system. These different types cater to varying requirements and scenarios in the Anti Submarine Rocket Launch System market.</p></p>
