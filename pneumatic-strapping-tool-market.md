<p><p>The pneumatic strapping tool market consists of two main types: automatic and semi-automatic. Automatic pneumatic strapping tools are equipped with advanced features that allow them to autonomously perform strapping operations, reducing the need for manual intervention. These tools are convenient for high-volume applications and are time-efficient. On the other hand, semi-automatic pneumatic strapping tools require some manual effort to complete the strapping process. They offer flexibility and control to the operator, making them suitable for a variety of strapping needs. Both types cater to different strapping requirements and are commonly used in industries like packaging, logistics, and construction.</p></p>
