<p><strong>The Radiation Processing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Isotope Radiation Processing</li><li>Accelerator Radiation Processing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Radiation processing market can be categorized into isotope radiation processing and accelerator radiation processing. Isotope radiation processing involves the use of radioactive isotopes, such as cobalt-60, to irradiate materials for various purposes like sterilization and food preservation. Accelerator radiation processing, on the other hand, employs high-energy electron beams or X-rays generated by particle accelerators to achieve similar outcomes. Both methods find applications in industries such as healthcare, agriculture, and manufacturing, where controlled exposure to radiation offers benefits like sterilization, cross-linking, and material modification.</p></p>
