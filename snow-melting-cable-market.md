These cables are energy-efficient and provide a higher level of safety. </p><p>Constant wattage type cables, on the other hand, have a fixed heat output regardless of the temperature. They are suitable for applications with higher power requirements and are less affected by variations in temperature. Both types offer effective snow melting solutions for different requirements and environments.</p></p>
